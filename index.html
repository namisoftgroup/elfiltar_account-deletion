Account Deletion Request - Elfiltar

Users of the Elfiltar app can request permanent deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to: filtarapp@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile information
  • Phone number
  • Messages and account activity
  • Any uploaded content associated with the account

Data that may be retained

Certain information may be retained if required by law or for fraud prevention purposes.

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
